Verification Data

Privacy Policy

TurboCanvas is a bridge, not a vault. We collect only the minimum data required to function:

  • Discord User ID: To map your request to the final image output.
  • Server ID: To ensure the bot responds in the correct channel.
  • Ephemeral Processing: All images are generated in temporary RAM and deleted after upload.

We do not sell data. We do not train models on your prompts. Everything stays on your machine.
